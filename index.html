Our Services

A&Ghafari Company excels in expert export and import services, ensuring seamless international trade operations without delays at customs.
Export & import of beans

We are a leading company specializing in the export and import industry, with a focus on legumes. With extensive experience and excellent technical knowledge, we are dedicated to providing and distributing high-quality legumes to various destinations worldwide. Some of the products we deal with include lentils, chickpeas, beans, white beans, and other superior quality legumes. Committed to international quality standards and efficiency, our goal is to deliver products with the highest industry standards, improve the sustainability of agricultural products, and establish effective and long-term business relationships with international customers and partners.
